Ertunc Aydogdu

I am a 5th-year Ph.D. candidate in Finance at the Marshall School of Business, University of Southern California. I am on the job market and available for interviews at the 2019 ASSA Conference at Atlanta.

My research explores how non-capital factors affect or interact with firms and capital markets. I focus on the intersection between finance and two important factors: (1) information environment, and (2) labor. My work generally falls into the category of empirical corporate finance.
